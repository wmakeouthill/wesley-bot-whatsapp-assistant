# Professional Experience - ANBIMA/Selic (Agreement with the Central Bank of Brazil)

## About ANBIMA

**ANBIMA (Associação Brasileira das Entidades dos Mercados Financeiro e de Capitais)** is the main association for the Brazilian financial market, representing financial institutions, asset managers, brokerages, distributors, and other capital market entities.

### Main Activities of ANBIMA

- **Regulation and Self-Regulation:** Develops and enforces conduct standards and best practices for the financial market
- **Professional Certifications:** Offers market-recognized certifications (CPA-10, CPA-20, CEA, etc.)
- **Education and Training:** Promotes financial education and professional development
- **Institutional Representation:** Represents the sector in dialogues with regulators and government
- **Research and Studies:** Produces studies and research on the Brazilian financial market

## About Selic (Special System for Settlement and Custody)

**Selic** is the system operated by the Central Bank of Brazil for recording, custody, and settlement of transactions with federal government bonds. It is one of the most critical systems in Brazil’s financial infrastructure.

### Main Functions of Selic

- **Custody of Bonds:** Safekeeping and administration of federal government bonds
- **Settlement of Transactions:** Processing and settlement of bond transactions
- **Trade Registration:** Registration of all transactions performed in the system
- **Selic Rate:** Basis for calculating Brazil’s benchmark interest rate
- **Critical Infrastructure:** Essential system for the functioning of the financial market

### Importance in the Financial System

- Critical system for the Brazilian economy
- Basis for the benchmark interest rate (Selic Rate)
- Essential infrastructure for the government bonds market
- Operated and supervised by the Central Bank of Brazil

## About the Central Bank of Brazil (BCB)

The **Central Bank of Brazil** is the monetary authority and regulator of the national financial system, responsible for ensuring price stability and the soundness and efficiency of the financial system.

### Main Responsibilities

- **Monetary Policy:** Controlling inflation and price stability
- **Financial Regulation:** Supervising and regulating financial institutions
- **Payment Systems:** Operating and supervising payment systems
- **Reserve Management:** Managing international reserves
- **Currency Issuance:** Controlling currency issuance

## Agreement Context

The work is carried out through an **agreement between ANBIMA and the Central Bank of Brazil**, enabling participation in projects involving Brazil’s financial infrastructure, especially related to Selic and coordination across market entities.
